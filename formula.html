The Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) club at Binghamton is a student-run organization dedicated to designing and building high-performance vehicles. The Formula team has has created several Formula-1 inspired vehicles over the years. This subset of the organization got their start with internal combustion engines, but have since made the switch to electric-drive. As a member of the Formula team, I have gained a lot of hands-on experience in engineering design and teamwork.
